# Redaction Note

These public documents are derived from internal architecture notes.

The following have been removed or generalized:
- private network details
- local hostnames and service URLs
- device identifiers tied to the private lab
- secrets, credentials, tokens, and pairing data

The goal is to keep the architecture public and useful without exposing the private environment it was developed in.

Public contact information may still be published where appropriate, including the email you choose to expose.
